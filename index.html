How It Works

Step 1: Get Verified

Meet with a notary to certify your identity and link it to your Stacks wallet.

Step 2: Upload Certification

Upload a digitized version of the notarized document to our website.

Step 3: Write and Sign Contracts

Draft your contracts on our platform and invite others to sign.

Step 4: Blockchain Security

All signatures are stored securely on the Stacks blockchain.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the on-chain notary service ensure security?

We use the Stacks blockchain, secured by Bitcoin, to store and verify all contracts and signatures.

What happens if my wallet is compromised?

If your wallet is compromised, notify us immediately and update your certification with a new notarized document.

Can anyone sign a contract on this platform?

Only verified users can sign contracts, ensuring that all signatures are genuine.

Is my personal information safe?

Your personal information is protected and used only to link your identity to your Stacks wallet.

About Us

We envision a world where decentralized legal agreements are the norm, allowing individuals and businesses to operate with greater security and efficiency.
